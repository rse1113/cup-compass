# IP Candidates — Flag for Legal Review

Logged per the standing portfolio rule to flag design patent and trademark candidates.
None of these have been filed or searched for conflicts — they are flagged for the user
to evaluate, not legal advice.

## Trademark candidates

- **"Cup Compass"** — brand name / wordmark.
- **"Find Your Bloom"** — tagline, used in the logo lockup, meta descriptions, and CTA copy.
- **"Bloom Score"** — the proprietary 0–100 scoring term used throughout the product
  (`/matches`, product cards, About page). This is the strongest candidate — it's used as
  a distinct branded term for a specific scoring methodology, not a generic descriptor.
- **The compass-rose + coffee-cup combined mark** (`src/components/Logo.jsx`,
  `public/favicon.svg`) — the specific graphic lockup, as a design/logo mark.

## Design patent candidates

- **"The Pour" radial-fill animation** (`src/components/ThePour/ThePour.jsx`) — the
  specific visual treatment of rendering a score as a circular pour-over dripper with an
  animated radial fill + bloom pulse. The novelty would be in the specific ornamental
  design (dripper-cone motif + bloom-pulse animation tied to a score), not the general
  idea of a radial progress indicator (which is not patentable on its own).

## Recommendation

Before any public launch or marketing spend, run a clearance search (USPTO TESS for word
marks, a design patent/trade-dress search for "The Pour") and confirm no conflicting
existing marks — especially for "Bloom Score," which is closest to functioning as a
standalone brand asset.
